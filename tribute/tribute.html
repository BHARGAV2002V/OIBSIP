Tribute to Virat Kohli

Introduction

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an Indian cricketer and one of the greatest batsmen of all time. He has earned numerous accolades and records throughout his cricketing career.

Early Life

Virat Kohli was born in Delhi, India. He showed a keen interest in cricket from a young age and began playing organized cricket in his early teens. He quickly rose through the ranks and caught the attention of selectors.

Cricket Career

Kohli made his international debut for India in August 2008. Since then, he has been a mainstay in the Indian batting lineup across all formats. He became the captain of the Indian cricket team in Test cricket in 2014 and later led the team in all formats. Kohli's elegant batting style, consistency, and aggressive approach have earned him admiration from fans and fellow cricketers worldwide.

Major Achievements

  • Recipient of numerous awards, including the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy (ICC Cricketer of the Year) multiple times.
  • Holds several records, including the fastest century in ODI cricket by an Indian.
  • Named as one of Time magazine's 100 most influential people in the world in 2018.

Personal Life

Virat Kohli is married to Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma. The couple tied the knot in December 2017. They have a daughter named Vamika, born in January 2021.

Timeline of Milestones

  • 2008: Made his international debut in an ODI against Sri Lanka.
  • 2010: Scored his first international century in an ODI against Sri Lanka.
  • 2014: Became the captain of the Indian Test cricket team.
  • 2018: Achieved the top spot in the ICC ODI batting rankings.
